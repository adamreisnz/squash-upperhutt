Keen to start playing? Choose from one of our annual membership options or sign up as a casual member.

Annual membership options

Senior Unrestricted playing rights. Can be graded and play interclub and all tournaments. $326
Junior Can be graded and play interclub and all tournaments. Must be under 19 years of age and not in full time employment. $99
Couple / Family Must all be living at same address. Membership fee is calculated at 100% of the price for the most expensive membership, then each subsequent membership discounted by 20% for 2nd member, 30% for the 3rd member, 40% for the 4th member and 50% for all other members. varies
Student / Apprentice Full-time tertiary student or working as a full-time apprentice (proof required). Unrestricted playing times and right to play interclub and tournaments. $194
Off Peak Monday to Friday up to 5.15pm and after 8.15pm, Saturday and Sunday all day. Unable to play interclub or graded tournaments. $189
Weekend All day Saturday and Sunday. Unable to play interclub or graded tournaments. $141
Associate Must be full financial member of another squash club. $136
Non Playing No playing access. Able to use club facilities, e.g. showers, bar, kitchen. $42

*Peak hours are Monday to Friday 5.15pm to 8.15pm

Casual membership options

Casual membership Flexible monthly membership with unrestricted playing times. Unable to play interclub or graded tournaments. $39 per month
Pay to play Pay per session, unrestricted playing times. Unable to play interclub or graded tournaments. $10 per session
Visitors Visitors coming to play with a member. $10 per session
